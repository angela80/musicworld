Jennifer Lopez

Jennifer Lopez is currently in the middle of her Las Vegas residency: All I Have, so of course (between her gigs, love life, and motherhood) she's super busy! If for some reason you're a huge JLover but don't have the money or time to catch her show in at the Planet Hollywood in Vegas, there's a way you can see it on the small screen and from the comfort of your own home. You see, the 48-year-old singer has just been announced as one of the performers at the 2018 Billboard Latin Music Awards. According to an official press release, she's going to "give a special performance" which only makes us believe that her show will be a preview of what fans can expect in her residency show. After all, the awards show IS taking place in Vegas, very close to her residency—makes total sense! If that's not enough for you to go cray, in addition to JLo's performance, artists such as Daddy Yankee, Luis Fonsi (remember Despacito?), Colombian heartthrob Maluma, Latin boyband of the moment, CNCO, and the new queen of hip-hop, Cardi B, will all be there! Don't miss out on this star-studded event at 7 p.m. ET on Thursday, April 26.

2018 MTV Video Music Awards

The 2018 MTV Video Music Awards are to be held on August 20, 2018 at Radio City Music Hall in New York City. The 35th annual award show will air live from the venue for the 12th time, the most of any previous venue in its history. Cardi B, who leads the list of nominations with eleven mentions, will serve as the show opener
